About Fish Hawk

Not just a neighborhood.
A way of life.

Fish Hawk is a census-designated place (CDP) in eastern Hillsborough County, encompassing communities like FishHawk Ranch, Fish Hawk Trails, and surrounding rural land. It's where the suburban conveniences of Tampa give way to something rarer — river access, open land, equestrian properties, and real breathing room.

The Outdoors

Florida the way it used to be

Fish Hawk sits alongside one of the most pristine natural corridors in Hillsborough County. The Alafia River, Alderman Ford Conservation Park, and Alafia River State Park give residents outdoor access that most Tampa Bay suburbs simply can't match.

🏞️

Alderman Ford Conservation Park

Nearly 1,700 acres of protected land along the Alafia River. Named after James Alderman who built a ford here in 1848, the park features hiking trails, river access, and some of the most unspoiled natural scenery in all of Hillsborough County.

Hiking · River Access · Wildlife
🛶

The Alafia River

The Alafia runs right through the Fish Hawk area — one of Florida's most beloved rivers for canoeing, kayaking, and freshwater fishing. Lithia Springs Park sits right on the river with a natural spring-fed swimming hole open year round.

Kayaking · Fishing · Swimming
🚵

Alafia River State Park

Over 20 miles of mountain biking trails, equestrian areas, hiking, camping, and river access — right in Fish Hawk's backyard. One of Florida's premier mountain biking destinations and a year-round destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Mountain Biking · Equestrian · Camping
🎯

FishHawk Sporting Clays

A world-class sporting clays facility right in the community and one of the finest in the Tampa Bay area. The kind of amenity you won't find in any other Hillsborough suburb — and a favorite of locals and visitors alike.

Sporting Clays · Recreation
⛳

River Hills Country Club

A private country club offering golf, tennis, swimming, and fitness. The course winds through the natural landscape alongside the Alafia River corridor — one of the most scenic settings for golf in the region.

Golf · Tennis · Fitness
🌿

More Conservation Land

Chito Branch Reserve, Keysville Park, and Osprey Ridge Park are all within easy reach. The area's deep commitment to conservation means the natural character of Fish Hawk is protected for generations to come.

Trails · Conservation · Open Space

Education

One of Florida's strongest school zones

Fish Hawk is served by Hillsborough County Public Schools — and its zone is widely considered one of the strongest in the state. Every level earns an A-rating from Florida, and most schools are located right inside the community.

The trifecta that draws families here: Fish Hawk is one of the only zones in all of Tampa Bay where elementary, middle, AND high school are all Florida A-rated. Bevis Elementary has been ranked the #1 elementary school in Hillsborough County and consistently places among the top schools statewide.
