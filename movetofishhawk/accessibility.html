Site Accessibility

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Our Commitment

Move to Fish Hawk (movetofishhawk.com) and Swift Home Mortgage are committed to ensuring this website is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

Measures We Take

We work to make this site accessible by applying the following practices. This site uses semantic HTML to provide meaningful structure for assistive technologies. Images include descriptive alternative text. Color contrast ratios are maintained at levels that support readability for users with low vision. Navigation is designed to be operable via keyboard. Forms include labeled fields to support screen reader compatibility.

Known Limitations

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of this site. Some content or features may not yet fully conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. We are actively working to identify and address any gaps.

Third-Party Content

This site may link to or reference content hosted by third parties. We cannot guarantee the accessibility of external sites or services, including the NMLS Consumer Access Portal or other linked resources.
