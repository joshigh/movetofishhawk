Fish Hawk Schools: A Complete Pipeline of A-Rated Public Schools

In a region where finding one A-rated public school in your zone is considered a win, Fish Hawk sits in a position most Tampa Bay communities can't match — a complete pipeline from Pre-K through 12th grade.

If you've ever talked to someone who moved to Fish Hawk, it usually doesn't take long before the conversation turns to the schools. In a region where finding one A-rated public school in your zone is considered a win, Fish Hawk sits in a position most Tampa Bay communities can't match — a complete pipeline of A-rated public schools from Pre-K through 12th grade, most of them either inside the community or within a couple miles of it.

Elementary Schools

Fish Hawk is served by three elementary schools, all carrying A ratings from the Florida Department of Education.

Bevis Elementary School sits at 5720 Osprey Ridge Drive inside Phase 2 of Fish Hawk Ranch and is one of the most decorated public elementary schools in the state. U.S. News ranks Bevis 26th among all elementary schools in Florida, with 91% of students proficient in math and 86% in reading — well above both Hillsborough County and state averages. The school has earned Blue Ribbon status along with Reading, ESE, and Math Teacher of the Year honors, and has been consistently recognized as a Top Performer on Florida's state assessments. Families in the Osprey Park and surrounding villages can walk their kids to school from a number of streets nearby.

Fishhawk Creek Elementary is located at 16815 Dorman Road on the eastern side of the community. It serves that corridor of Fish Hawk and carries its own strong academic record — an A-rated school with a GreatSchools score of 8 out of 10 and test scores consistently above district and state averages.

Stowers Elementary at 13915 Barrington Stowers Drive is the newest of the three, sitting directly inside Fish Hawk Ranch West. Its location makes it a genuine neighborhood school — walkable for many residents of that section of the community, and already earning strong marks since opening.

Middle Schools

Randall Middle School sits at 16510 Fish Hawk Blvd along the main boulevard on the north side of the road, right next to Newsome High School. It serves a large portion of the Fish Hawk community and has consistently held A ratings from the state.

Barrington Middle School at 5925 Village Center Drive is tucked inside Fish Hawk Ranch West alongside Stowers Elementary. The two schools sit close enough together that families in that section of the community have a school path from kindergarten through 8th grade without ever really leaving the neighborhood — a practical advantage that gets noticed by parents during the relocation decision.

Newsome High School

Everything in Fish Hawk feeds into Joe E. Newsome High School at 16550 Fish Hawk Blvd, right next door to Randall Middle and sitting along the same campus corridor.

Newsome is one of the top high schools in Florida by most measures. U.S. News ranks it 45th in the state, and 72% of students participate in Advanced Placement coursework. In the 2024-25 school year, 81% of 10th-grade students scored proficient or better in English Language Arts, compared to 57% for Hillsborough County and 58% for the state. Four-year graduation rates have consistently outperformed both district and state averages.

The school's marching band has performed in the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade, and Newsome has received recognition for an Elite Athletic Program from Hillsborough County. Students also have access to the Bright Futures Scholarship Program, which can cover 75% to 100% of public college tuition at Florida colleges and universities — a meaningful long-term financial benefit for families thinking about the cost of higher education.

Fish Hawk is one of the very few zones in the Tampa Bay area where every level — elementary, middle, and high school — carries an A-rating, with most schools located either inside the community or within two and a half miles of it. For families relocating to the Tampa area and weighing their options, that kind of consistency across every grade level is genuinely hard to find.

One important note: school zone assignment is determined by your specific street address, not the neighborhood name. Two homes a block apart can be zoned for different elementary schools. Always verify your zone through the HCPS School Locator before making a purchase decision where a specific school matters.
